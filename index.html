Kennedy House Community Resources

Hey folks! In order to more efficiently serve our clients, the placement students at Kennedy House have been tasked with putting together a binder of Community Resources to keep at the shelter.

We know that there are tons of amazing community partners offering services that can greatly benefit our youth clients, and we want to collect them all in one place for your convenience!

Please provide as much detail as you can (including names of any contacts you may know at the agency/organization). If you would like to add more than one resource, just click: Add Resource
Add as many resources as you would like, and come back anytime to add more as you think of them!
